Futsal

Playing

I have been playing futsal since I was 6 years old. I can play as a field player or as a goalkeeper, I don't really have a preference. I also played football for a long time but these sports are as different as complementary.

The biggest difference between these two sports is the size of the field. When we play football, we have a very large field. So we can use our speed to dribble past a player even if we are not good with the ball. We can simply push the ball and run faster than our opponent for several meters.

When we play futsal we don't have this possibility, so the technique with the ball is more important. Since there are fewer dribbling opportunities, team play is also very important.

Unlike football where we play with a team of 11 players, in futsal we only play with 5 players by team. Since we have fewer dribbling opportunities, less space on the pitch and fewer teammates, the key to futsal is movement and explosiveness.

This is why this sport is more spectacular and has less downtime than its cousin football.

Coaching

Last year I decided to start my own futsal club. Having started coaching young boys aged 5-6 two years ago has taught me that I enjoy teaching others and managing a team.

So I created a futsal team and over time I noticed enthusiasm and a strong demand for a women's team. Before the start of the season, I also decided to create this team and the club has now 2 teams.

I play on the men's team and coach the women's team. The pleasure is totally different but just as great. Coaching is more a question of explanations, humans relations, detection of the strength and weakness of each player, ...

As I don't like doing things without knowledge, I took coaching courses with Adeps. I have almost 4 certificates on football and futsal training (1 of which is in progress).

Managing

Running a club takes a lot of time but I try to do my best by doing it professionally even though it is an amateur club.

I created a website to display all the information about the club, the teams, the sponsors, ... and also allow players to manage their availability for each match or training.

I also manage the team's social network with a Facebook page.

I try to take as much responsibility as possible in the management of the club so that the players can concentrate on the pleasure of practicing their favorite sport.
